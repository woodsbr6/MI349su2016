Modularity

Instagram is an example of “Modularity” because it divides systems into self contained systems, easy to navigate and is less prone to error. Also allows users to create custom solutions, their own profile name, own profile picture, and own biography description.

Hick's Law

AMC Theater website is an example of “Hick’s Law” because it has limited functions, pick a movie, pick show time, pay online or at theater. Hick’s Law is when the time it takes to make a decision increases as the number of alternatives increases. AMC has a few functions so theres only a few decisions to be made.

80/20 Rule

Google is an example of the “80/20 Rule” because it is all about the user, the users have to send a command ignored for google to be useful, 80% of the website usage comes from 20% of visitors.

Rule of Thirds

The rolling stone image is an example of “Rule of Thirds” because the page is literally cut into thirds. There are also lines on the image to express the flow of the page in its thirds.

Ockham's Razor

Jets pizza website is an example of “Ockham’s Razor” because it is a simple website and very clear on the functions. You have the choice of ordering online for delivery or pick up, pick you type of food and done.
